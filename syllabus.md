# Syllabus (2 × 45 min)

1. **Descriptive statistics & visualisation**  
   Mean/median/mode; hist/bar/pie/boxplot; outliers & robustness.
2. **Probability & CLT**  
   Binomial, Poisson, Normal; sampling distributions; intuition.
3. **Hypothesis testing & p-values (simulation-first)**  
   Courtroom analogy; empirical p-values; one-/two-sided.
4. **Errors, Power & Sample Size**  
   Type I/II, power curves; planning n; margin of error.
5. **Parametric vs Non-parametric**  
   Assumptions; t-tests vs Wilcoxon/Mann-Whitney; discrete data.
6. **Multiple testing & Effect size**  
   Familywise error; Bonferroni; Cohen’s d, correlation r.

Each session ends with **Quick Checks** in `exercises/`.
